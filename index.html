Proprietary vs. Open-Source Systems in Robotics Education: A Comparative Study on Open Architecture and Hardware Transparency

Authors: Gaurav Mall Tarlok

Published at: Υβριδική Διημερίδα 2026 «Πιλοτικές - Ερευνητικές κατευθύνσεις και προοπτικές των Επιστημών στην Δ/θμια Εκπαίδευση»

DOI: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18749090

Abstract: This study evaluates the financial efficiency and educational efficacy of "White Box" open-architecture systems (like OhmMyBox) against proprietary "Black Box" ecosystems. Findings suggest that open-architecture systems offer deeper mechanical and electronic understanding for secondary education students while reducing acquisition costs.
